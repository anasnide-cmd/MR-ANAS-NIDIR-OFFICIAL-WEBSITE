## üèóÔ∏è 1. Building Your First Site (MR BUILD)

MR BUILD makes it easy to go from an idea to a live website in minutes.

### Step A: The Dashboard

1.  Navigate to **MR BUILD**.
2.  Click **"CREATE NEW SITE"**.
3.  Enter your **Site Name** and a unique **Slug** (this will be your URL, e.g., `/s/my-awesome-site`).

### Step B: Using Magic Build (AI Assistant)

1.  Once in the Editor, look for the **Magic Build AI** sidebar on the right.
2.  In the chat box, describe what you want.
    - _Example: "Make a dark-themed hero section for a futuristic music festival with a glowing 'Buy Tickets' button."_
3.  The AI will generate the code. Once it's ready, click **"APPLY TO CANVAS"**.
4.  Your site preview will update instantly!

### Step C: Fine-Tuning

1.  Switch to the **"CODE"** tab to manually adjust HTML or CSS.
2.  Use the **"TEMPLATES"** tab to quickly inject pre-designed sections.
3.  Click **"SAVE PROJECT"** to keep your progress.
